Travel Options

Discover different ways to reach your destination!

By Air

Traveling by air is a convenient and fast way to reach your destination. Check out the nearest airports and flight options.

  • Pokhara:

    • Nearest Airport: Pokhara Airport
    • Direct Flights: Available from Tribhuvan International Airport (KTM)

  • Chitwan:

    • Nearest Airport: Bharatpur Airport
    • Direct Flights: Available from Tribhuvan International Airport (KTM)

  • Mustang:

    • Nearest Airport: Jomsom Airport
    • Direct Flights: Available from Tribhuvan International Airport (KTM)

By Road

Exploring the roadways can be an exciting adventure. Find information on bus services, highways, and scenic routes.

  • Pokhara:

    • Highway: Prithvi Highway (Kathmandu to Pokhara)
    • Distance from Kathmandu: Approximately 200 km
    • Bus Services: Regular bus services available

  • Chitwan:

    • Highway: Narayangadh-Muglin Road (Kathmandu to Chitwan)
    • Distance from Kathmandu: Approximately 150 km
    • Bus Services: Regular bus services available

  • Mustang:

    • Scenic Route: Annapurna Circuit Trek (for adventurous travelers)
    • Distance from Kathmandu: Varies depending on the trekking route
    • Trekking Guide: Recommended for Mustang trek
